ABOUT THE BARANGAY

THE CANLUBANG

Barangay Canlubang is a vibrant and thriving community located in the heart of Calamba, Laguna. Known for its rich cultural heritage and rapid development, Canlubang is home to a diverse population dedicated to fostering progress, inclusivity, and sustainability.

Over the years, Barangay Canlubang has become a hub for innovation and growth. With strong partnerships between local leaders, residents, and various sectors, the barangay continues to address community challenges while promoting advancements in education, technology, and infrastructure.

As a forward-looking barangay, Canlubang remains committed to creating opportunities for its citizens, embracing the spirit of collaboration, and paving the way for a better future. Together, we are building a connected, empowered, and resilient community that thrives on shared values and collective success.

MCDC of Canlubang

The Blockchain Barangay initiative, under the guidance of MCDC Canlubang, aims to revolutionize local governance by implementing cutting-edge blockchain technology. This platform will enhance transparency, streamline services, and ensure secure management of community records, bringing forth a new era of digital governance in Canlubang.
